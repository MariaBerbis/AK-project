Why Alicia Keys

Why is Alicia Keys the best?

It's unusual for an artist to be so multi-talented. Alicia Keys is a classically trained pianist and started composing when she was 12 and signed at 15 years old by Columbia Records.

Her debut album, Songs in A Minor, sold over 12 million copies worldwide and earned Keys 5 Grammy Awards in 2002. Best New Artist and Song of the Year for "Fallin" are a few of her accolades. She is also the second American artist ever to receive five Grammys in a single night. All of her albums except for her fourth, The Element of Freedom, peaked at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 in the US.

Alicia Keys is a 15-time Grammy winner and has sold over 90 million records worldwide.
